Honor

Honor is the measure of Arthur's moral standing based on your actions. Honor will increase with actions that show kindness, such as donating to camp, assisting strangers that are injured or stranded in the wild, turning down duels, ect. Actions that will decrease honor include stealing, murdering, antagonizing people, and other unpleasant actions. Honor affects how other characters in the game treat Arthur, and how much shopkeepers charge for items.
